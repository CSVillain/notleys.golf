Golf at The Notleys

The Notleys is an 18-hole, 6,116-yard par 71 parkland course set in the valley of the River Brain. The layout offers wide views across the Essex countryside, with steady elevation changes and several holes influenced by the river. It provides a fair test for both regular players and visitors, combining accessibility with enough challenge to keep the round interesting.

18 holes Parkland layout
Par 71 Steady elevation changes
6,116 yards River Brain valley setting
