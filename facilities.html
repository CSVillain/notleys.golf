Facilities

Facilities are available around daily course conditions, coaching schedules, and clubhouse opening times.

Driving range

Covered bays give you a straightforward place to warm up properly or spend time working on your swing without rushing.

  • Ideal for pre-round warm-ups and dialling in longer clubs.
  • Easy to combine with short-game practice in the same visit.
  • Range balls are sold in 20-ball increments at 12.5p per ball.
  • Example: 20 balls £2.50 / 40 balls £5.00 / 60 balls £7.50.
  • Availability can vary around coaching sessions and daily conditions.

Chipping green

A simple, practical area for short-game work just a short walk from the clubhouse.

  • Useful for chip-and-run, lofted shots, and landing control.
  • Works well alongside the putting green for a full short-game routine.
  • Maintained regularly, so access may vary during upkeep.

Putting green

A calm space next to the clubhouse to get a feel for the greens before heading out.

  • Best for pace control and short putting routines.
  • Convenient for a quick warm-up before teeing off.
  • Open alongside normal course preparation and weather conditions.

Coaching

Golf lessons are available with our PGA-qualified Golf Professional, David Bugg.

  • Individual 30-minute lesson: £30.
  • Five 30-minute lessons: £120.
  • Lesson vouchers are available.
  • Equipment can be provided.

Buggy hire

Single-seater buggies are available for £25, subject to course conditions and daily availability.

  • Single-seater only due to bridges and the railway tunnel on the course.
  • Up to 15 may be available, but numbers can vary.
  • Best arranged in advance, especially during busy periods.

Clubhouse

The clubhouse is open to visitors, members, and guests before and after a round.

  • All visitors must check in before starting their round.
  • Food and drink available to all.
  • Terraced seating with views of the 18th green and the valley.
  • Opening times vary by season, with summer hours typically 7am–10pm.
