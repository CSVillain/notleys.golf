Course Guide

The River Brain valley in one round

The Notleys is an 18-hole, par 71 parkland course with open views, steady elevation changes, and a routing that stays enjoyable for regular players and first-time visitors alike.

Course Overview

Set in the valley of the River Brain, the layout combines broad views with a sequence of holes that rise, fall, and turn enough to stay interesting across the full round. It is approachable from the tee, but still asks for tidy approaches and solid distance control into the greens.

18 holes Parkland routing
Par 71 Playable for visitors and members
6,116 yards Valley setting with elevation change
