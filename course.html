Course Guide

A proper look at the round before you arrive

The Notleys is a par 71 parkland course in the River Brain valley. Expect open views, measured elevation changes, and a layout that stays playable for visitors while still asking for control into the greens.

Overview

A straightforward course guide, not a pile of uploads

The course sits neatly in the valley and unfolds with a clear rhythm: generous views from the tee, changing levels across the routing, and approach shots that matter more than brute force. It is enjoyable on a first visit and familiar enough to reward repeat play.

Open sightlines The shape of each hole reads clearly from the tee.
Measured climbs and falls Elevation keeps the round moving without becoming punishing.
Playable routing Visitors can settle in quickly and still find enough challenge.
